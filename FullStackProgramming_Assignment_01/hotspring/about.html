Our Story

HotSpring Portable Spas has been the nation's leading retailer of hot tubs, portable spas, and outdoor recreation products for over 30 years. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional quality, outstanding customer service, and the best prices on the market.

Founded in 1990, we started as a small family business with a single showroom in New York. Today, we operate multiple locations across the country and serve thousands of satisfied customers nationwide through our state-of-the-art online store.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: provide our customers with the finest hot tubs and portable spas at the most competitive prices, backed by unmatched customer service and technical support. We believe every family deserves the luxury of hydrotherapy and outdoor relaxation.

🌐 Nationwide Delivery

We deliver to all 50 states with white-glove delivery service available. Our professional installation teams ensure your spa is set up correctly and ready to enjoy.

✅ Quality Guarantee

Every product we sell comes with manufacturer warranties and our own satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy, neither are we. Period.

👥 Expert Support

Our team of certified spa technicians is available 7 days a week to answer your questions, provide maintenance advice, and ensure your spa runs perfectly.

💰 Best Price Guarantee

We guarantee the lowest prices on all brands we carry. Found a better price? Let us know and we'll match it — no questions asked.

Our Brands

We carry the finest spa brands available including HotSpring, Caldera Spas, Island Spas, and Oceanic Spa. Each brand has been carefully selected for quality, reliability, and innovation.
